What a great real estate Notion template includes

1. Lead tracker

Every prospect deserves a row in your lead tracker — their name, phone, email, budget, source, current status and next follow-up date. Hot leads, warm leads, cold leads — filter and sort to know exactly who to call today. Never let a lead go cold because you forgot to follow up.

2. Property listings

All your active, pending and sold listings in one database. Track address, price, bedrooms, days on market, client and commission at a glance. Board view shows listings by status. Calendar view shows upcoming open houses. Filter to see only your active listings in seconds.

3. Deal pipeline

Every deal moving through stages from initial meeting to closed commission. Kanban board view lets you see your entire pipeline at a glance — how many deals in showing, how many under contract, how many closing this month. This is the page you check every morning.

4. Open house manager

Track every open house — date, property, number of attendees, leads generated and follow-up status. The agents who win are the ones who follow up with every attendee within 24 hours. This page makes sure that never slips.

5. Commission tracker

Know exactly what you're owed and when. Every commission logged — pending, received and projected. Review this every Friday to understand your monthly income and plan ahead. No more surprises at the end of the month.
