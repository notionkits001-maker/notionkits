Best Notion Template for Nurses in 2026

Published June 2026 · 5 min read · NotionKits.co

Nursing is one of the most demanding professions in the world. On a busy ward you're managing multiple patients, administering medications, writing handover notes, tracking CPD hours for revalidation and planning your rota — all while providing high-quality patient care. Paper notes get lost. Memory fails at the end of a 12-hour shift. There has to be a better way.

A well-built Notion template gives nurses a personal digital system to stay organized — not a replacement for hospital systems, but a personal tool that helps you stay on top of everything that matters.

Important note

This template is a personal organization tool — not a replacement for your hospital's official patient management system. Always follow your hospital's clinical protocols. Use this alongside official systems to stay personally organized.

What a great nurse Notion template includes

1. Patient care log

Every patient on your ward — their room, condition, priority level and next check time. Sort by priority at the start of your shift to know who needs attention first. Update next check time after every patient contact. Use patient IDs instead of full names to keep it HIPAA-friendly.

2. Medication log

Every dose given — medication, dose, route, time scheduled, time given and verifying nurse. Log every medication immediately after giving it — never from memory at the end of the shift. Include the verifying nurse for controlled drugs. This is your personal record, separate from the official MAR.

3. Shift handover

Clean structured handovers with critical alerts, pending tasks and patient updates for the incoming nurse. Fill in handover notes 30 minutes before your shift ends — not in the last 5 minutes. A good handover protects your patients and your colleagues. Use SBAR format: Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation.

4. CPD tracker

Track every training course, workshop and online module for revalidation. Most nursing boards require 35-40 CPD hours per year. Log every course here — even short online modules count. Review monthly so you're never scrambling at revalidation time. Store certificate details and expiry dates.

5. Shift schedule

Your rota planned weeks ahead. Know your morning, afternoon and night shifts in advance so you can organize your personal life around them. Share with family so they know your working pattern. Plan rest days and self-care around your busiest shifts.
