Do I need a Notion account?

Yes — Notion is free to sign up at notion.so. The free Notion plan is enough to use all our templates. You don't need a paid Notion subscription.

What exactly do I get in the premium kit?

A fully built Notion workspace file you duplicate with one click. It comes pre-filled with example data so you see exactly how it works, with multiple views already set up — board, calendar, table — plus a PDF setup guide and a built-in how-to-use page inside the workspace.

Can I preview all kits for free?

Yes — all 6 kit previews are completely free with no signup needed. Click any niche to see the pages and database structure inside each kit before buying.

What if my job isn't listed?

Email us at notionkits001@gmail.com and we'll build a custom kit for your niche. New kits are added regularly — Content Creator, ADHD, Student and Nurse kits are all available now.

Is this a subscription?

No. Previewing kits is always free. Each premium kit is a one-time payment — $17 to $37 depending on the niche. You own it forever and get all future updates at no extra cost.
