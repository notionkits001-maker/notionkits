Why Airbnb hosts are switching to Notion

Dedicated property management software like Guesty or Lodgify costs $50-200 per month. For hosts with 1-5 properties that's a significant expense that eats into margins. Notion gives you 80% of the functionality for a one-time cost — and it's flexible enough to adapt to your exact operation.

What a great Airbnb Notion template includes

1. Property listings database

Every property with nightly rate, weekend rate, cleaning fee, platform, rating and total reviews. See all your properties at a glance and compare performance. Know instantly which properties are your stars and which need attention. Update pricing seasonally from one database rather than logging into multiple platforms.

2. Bookings tracker

Every reservation from confirmed to completed. Guest name, check-in, check-out, net revenue after platform fees, special requests and notes. The special requests column is the most important — dietary needs, early check-in requests, pack n play for a toddler. Missing these destroys your rating. One bad review from a forgotten special request can hurt bookings for months.

3. Cleaning and maintenance

Every turnover scheduled the moment a booking is confirmed. Every maintenance task logged with cost, assignee and status. Your cleaner is your most important business partner — give them maximum notice. A missed turnover is the single worst thing that can happen to your listing. This database prevents it.

4. Finance tracker

Every income and expense logged — booking revenue, cleaning costs, maintenance, insurance, platform fees, supplies. Most Airbnb expenses are tax deductible. Keep this database updated and your tax preparation becomes a 30-minute job instead of a stressful weekend scramble.

5. Guest reviews

Every review logged with rating, summary and response status. Respond to every review within 24 hours — especially negative ones. Future guests read your responses. A professional, calm response to a 3-star review often wins more trust than 10 generic 5-star reviews.
